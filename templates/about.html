About ResyMatch

Our Mission

ResyMatch was created to streamline the hiring process by using natural language processing and machine learning to match job descriptions with the most suitable resumes.

Our goal is to save recruiters and HR professionals valuable time by automatically identifying the best candidates for positions, allowing them to focus on the human aspects of hiring.

How It Works

ResyMatch uses Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency (TF-IDF) vectorization and cosine similarity to analyze and score resumes against job descriptions. This allows us to identify the resumes that most closely match the requirements and qualifications listed in the job posting.
